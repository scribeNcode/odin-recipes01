lasagna recipe

Photos of Homemade Lasagna

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling. A more traditional homemade lasagna filling would be made with ricotta but my mom's recipe calls for a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan. I have found none better anywhere. Serve with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. This lasagna recipe starts with a pound of ground meat (½ pound ground pork, ½ pound lean ground beef).
  2. A diced onion is cooked until translucent with the ground meat.
  3. You'll need a can of tomato sauce and a can of crushed tomatoes.
  4. For fresh flavor, chop two tablespoons of parsley and crush one clove of garlic.
  5. A dash of sugar balances out all of the acidity from the tomatoes.
  6. This homemade lasagna is seasoned with dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  7. you'll need lasagna noodles! This recipe calls for uncooked noodles, but you can use the oven-ready variety to save time.
  8. The cheese layer is made up of cottage cheese and Parmesan. You'll also need shredded mozzarella.
  9. Eggs make the cheese layer extra creamy. Plus, they act as a binding agent (which means they hold the layer together).
